From the Founder

I'm Abhishek.
This is Elviano.

Growing up in Jagdalpur, Bastar, I always had an eye for detail. After years in fashion, I wanted to create something slower — where each piece had a reason to exist.

Elviano began with a single screen printing table and a conviction that less could be more. Every edition is designed personally. Every print is pulled by hand. When a piece is gone, it's gone.

Abhishek Yadav
