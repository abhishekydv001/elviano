Our Story

Impressions
with Intention

Elviano was born from a simple belief—clothing should carry meaning. Every piece we create is a limited impression, never to be repeated.

The Elviano
Manifesto

"We believe in the beauty of limitation. That scarcity creates value. That a single, considered impression is worth more than a thousand copies. We print by hand. We release in editions. We never restock."
— Abhishek Yadav, Founder
From the Founder

I'm Abhishek.
This is Elviano.

Growing up in Jagdalpur, Bastar, I always had an eye for detail. After years in fashion, I wanted to create something slower. Something where each piece had a reason to exist.

Elviano began with a single screen printing table and a conviction that less could be more. Every edition is designed personally. Every print is pulled by hand. When a piece is gone, it's gone.

Abhishek Yadav
What We Stand For

Our Values

Intentional Design

Every print begins with a concept. No random graphics. Just ideas worth wearing.

Handmade Process

Screen printed by hand. Variations aren't flaws—they're signatures.

Limited Editions

Once sold out, screens are retired. What you wear becomes part of a closed set.

Responsible Production

Small batches. Deadstock fabrics when possible. No waste.

Indian Craft

Cut and sewn in India. Printed in-house. 100% made in India.

Built to Last

220 GSM minimum. Garments that age with grace.
